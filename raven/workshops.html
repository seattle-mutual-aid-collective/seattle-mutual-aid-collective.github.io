Seattle Mutual Aid Collective Resilience Workshop Series

Our core programming consists of a workshop series designed to build practical skills and distribute essential resources:

  1. Emergency First Aid Training
    • First aid training with the Stop-the-Bleed program
    • Accessibility of essential first aid supplies to vulnerable individuals
  2. Emergency Preparedness Workshops
    • Emergency kit and go-bag workshop with Pipsqueak Community Space
    • Community emergency planning and household resiliency preparation
    • Provide of emergency supply kits and go-bag supplies to individuals and households with limited resources
  3. Communications Resilience Training
    • Radio training workshop for emergency and disaster preparedness
    • Enhance community preparedness with accessible handheld battery-operated radios
    • Coordination with Seattle Emergency Communication Hubs and the Auxiliary Communication Service to practice communications when standard systems fail
  4. Climate Adaptation and Food Security
    • Scientist-led climate change resilience and adaptation workshop
    • Community gardening workshop by SMAC Seed Collective
    • Promotion of hyperlocal food security solutions
    • Contribution to diversity of local seed bank

All workshops will include targeted outreach to vulnerable and underserved communities who often face challenges accessing standard neighborhood-based resilience efforts.
